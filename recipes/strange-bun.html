Strange bun

Description

Strange Bun is a fluffy perfection with delicious bright pinkish purple filling. My dear friend Shane recently shared this recipe with me.

It's my favorite snack when I'm running errands in Stardew Valley, even though not many appreciate it as much as I do. Nothing beats 100 Energy and 45 Health in such a pretty form!

Ingredients

  • wheat flour
  • periwinkle
  • void mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
  2. Generously oil or spray a 12-cup muffin tin and set it aside.
  3. Gently crack the periwinkle shell and set it aside. You can grind the periwinkle shell and use it as a fertilizer or dye.
  4. In a medium bowl, mix periwinkle with void mayonnaise.
  5. Stir in the wheat flour. Spoon the mixture into the prepared muffin tin.
  6. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until golden brown.
  7. Enjoy!
